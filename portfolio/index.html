My first exposure to on-air reporting proved to be a transformational experience. I instantly fell in love with bringing news stories to life through clear oration, compelling live interviews, and informed commentary.

From 12:00 p.m. to 7:00 p.m., I spent the first half of my shifts creating politics and business segments for air. During this time, I pitched stories relevant to local L.A. audiences, gathered and edited visual content, conducted interviews and wrote scripts.

The second half of my shifts focused on delivering each story on air in the most effective way possible. I also pinpointed opportunities to provide unscripted commentary, adding context and depth to the story.
