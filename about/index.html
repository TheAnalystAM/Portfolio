About

I’m an early-career journalist on track to graduate summa cum laude from the University of Southern California this spring. With a double major in journalism and business administration, and internship experience across both news and business, I focus on breaking down the complex interactions between politics and markets through in-depth reporting.

As the line between business and politics grows increasingly blurred, exploring this intersection has never been more important. Global citizens must understand how fiscal decisions drive politics, and how politics, in turn, drives markets.

My reporting in this space has included coverage of Donald Trump Jr.’s use of his family’s political brand for financial gain, the European Union’s costly military rearmament poised to swell fiscal deficits and the ripple effects of Trump’s tariffs on Asian regional diplomacy aimed at containing China.

I’m now seeking internship and full-time opportunities that will allow me to strengthen my reporting skills, build a network of sources, and learn from accomplished journalists who can guide my professional growth.
